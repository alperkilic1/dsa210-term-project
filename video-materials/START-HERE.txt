INCIDENT GENOME - VIDEO MATERIALS
=================================

This folder has everything needed to record the optional project video.

The video is NOT required for submission. The official DSA210 guidelines say
the final report and code are required, and you "may additionally include a
video or project webpage." This is supplementary material that strengthens the
"presentation" grading criterion.


WHAT'S IN HERE
--------------
  Incident-Genome-Slides.pptx   9-slide deck. All figures are embedded. The
                                narration for each slide sits in its speaker
                                notes -- open the Notes pane in PowerPoint.

  narration-script.txt          The full narration as one continuous script,
                                slide by slide. ~370 words, ~2.5 minutes.

  recording-guide.html          Step-by-step recording, compression, and
                                hosting guide. Open it in any browser.


HOW TO MAKE THE VIDEO - 3 STEPS
-------------------------------
  1. Open Incident-Genome-Slides.pptx in PowerPoint. Read narration-script.txt
     aloud once to rehearse -- aim for under 3 minutes.

  2. PowerPoint > Slide Show > Record Slide Show. Narrate each slide and
     advance manually. The script is right there in the speaker notes.

  3. File > Export > Create a Video (720p is fine). If the file is over 2 MB,
     use the ffmpeg command in recording-guide.html to compress it.


WHERE THE VIDEO GOES
--------------------
  Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive, then paste the link into the
  final report. Or, if the compressed file is small, drop the .mp4 in this
  folder.

  Before recording: quiet room, Do Not Disturb on, do a 10-second test first.
  The full checklist is in recording-guide.html.
